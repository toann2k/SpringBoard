1) What is the differnce between var and let?

You can assign and redeclare var. You cannot redeclare let, in additon to that let create block scope. 




2) What is the differnce between var and const?

You can reassign and  redeclare with var, but you cannot declare or reassign using the const keyword. 



3) What is the differnce between let and const?

You can reassign with let, but you cannot redeclare or reassign using the const keyword. 


4) What is hoisting?

It javascript, a variable can be declared after it has been used. Using a let variable before it is declared 
will result in a ReferenceError.
